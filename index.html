Tips From An Insider

  • An Aklan State University - Bachelor of Science in Nursing student should be equipped with:
    K - Knowledge. It provides the theoretical foundation necessary to understand concepts and principles relevant to the program.
    S - Skills. These enable individuals to apply that knowledge effectively in practical tasks and situations.
    A - Attitude. In nursing, having the right attitude is paramount for providing high-quality care and fostering positive relationships with clinical instructors and other students.
